Cats vs Dogs

Liam and Krystal debate which animal is the superior house pet:
Cats or Dogs?

Krystal Thinks Cats!


Cats are clearly the better house pet, for many reasons...

  1. Cats for the most part are perfectly happy staying indoors.
    You won’t need to worry coming home early to let them outside!
    They actually might even prefer when you’re out…
  2. Having a pest problem? If you already have a cat you probably answered no to that question.
    Cats will keep all the mice and bugs away from your house!
  3. Other than when it’s time to eat, cats are far quieter than dogs. You won’t have to worry when
    the mail man stop by, cats won’t be causing a scene.
  4. Now, though it might be strange that your cat has a little house inside specifically to use the bathroom,
    you won’t have to go outside in the middle of winter like someone with a dog would.
  5. if there is one thing dogs love, it'r rolling in the mud!
    cats on the otherhand, hate it. They love staying clea, and eve give themselves their own baths.
For these reasons, cats make the best house pet!
- Krystal

Liam thinks dogs!


Dogs are clearly the superior house pet for many different reasons

  1. Dogs are very loyal animals and will always listen to their trainer as long as you are willing to train them
  2. They may prefer to go outside but that encourages activity in
    both pet and pet handler keeping both physically active
  3. Along with their loyalty comes comfort from the dog which is great for people who may need it.
    They can have dogs specific to their needs like guide dogs, seizure dogs and emotional support dogs
  4. In addition dogs can make for good guards to both property and people and help many people like fire fighters and police do their jobs.
  5. Dogs also don't need that much in the house unless you want to give them extra things.
    All they need is food and water bowls, their bed and a few toys. Anything else can easily be put outsied as they will love being out there anyways.
  6. A dog may have to go out in the middle of the night but that will help you feel less lazy and used to waking up
    This being up at random times of the night could also be helpful when you have kids who also do the same at a young age.
  7. Speaking of kids, dogs are very patient with kids and will let them do mostly anything to them without retalliatio as they know they are just a little curious.
    Now of course dogs will have their limits but will give pently of warning to the kid in order for them to not get hurt doing something they shouldn't
For these reasons I would say dogs make the better house pet
- Liam
